FAQ

Common
Questions

How long does an audit take?
Typically 7–14 business days depending on business complexity and the depth of data we need to analyze. We'll give you a precise timeline during the discovery call.
Will I need to share confidential information?
Yes. Deep audits require access to real data. All information shared is protected under a strict NDA and confidentiality agreement signed before any work begins.
Do you implement the recommendations?
Yes. Implementation support is available as a separate engagement. Many clients choose to receive the audit report first, then engage us for execution support on priority items.
Do you work with early-stage startups?
Absolutely. We work with businesses from the idea stage through growth stage. Early audits often surface the most significant opportunities before bad habits become expensive problems.
What makes you different from a marketing agency?
We have no financial incentive tied to your ad spend or channel selection. Our only incentive is to give you an honest, unbiased view of what's working and what isn't — and we put that in writing.
